
What is policy in S3?

- An S3 bucket policy is a JSON document that defines **permissions** for an Amazon S3 bucket and its objects. 

- **It specifies who (users, groups, or the public) can perform what actions (e.g., read, write, delete) on the bucket or its contents, under what conditions.**
